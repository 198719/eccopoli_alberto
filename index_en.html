Eccopoli, Public Policies, Human Rights and Durable Development

Let's work together



Discovering on time and fighting to guarantee the most important needs of the most vulnerable communities is vital to make a significant change.



We are a non-governmental organization created to identify these needs in communities in Haiti, Mexico and other countries in the region.

When we achieve our goal, we can eventually generate important changes in public policy issues.

Working with communities at a grassroots level, Eccopoli is an important actor in catalyzing change and progress through political and economic development..
